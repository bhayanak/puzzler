How to Play Flow Free

  • Goal: Connect all matching colored dots and fill 100% of the board
  • Drag to Connect: Click/tap on a colored circle and drag to its matching color
  • Paths Cannot Cross: Each path must be independent
  • Backtrack: Drag back over your path to undo mistakes
  • Clear Path: Click on any path to remove it completely
  • Win Condition: All cells filled AND all colored pairs connected
