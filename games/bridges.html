How to Play

  • Goal: Connect all islands with bridges
  • Rules: Each island shows how many bridges it needs (1-4)
  • Connect: Click one island, then click another in the same row/column
  • Multiple Bridges: Click same pair again for double bridge (max 2)
  • Remove: Click a third time to remove the bridge
  • Win: All islands must have the correct number of bridges
